About Us

The Community Resource Center of Las Vegas is an information platform designed to put valuable resources for individuals and families in one place within the Las Vegas Valley. Our website is an ongoing effort to provide timely information for services that can help you deal with everyday challenges. Whether in crisis or looking to improve your life, our goal is to make finding resources easier and less time-consuming for you. It is our commitment as a team to make available information on community programs, assistance services, and opportunities all in one place. We know that life isn't easy sometimes, but we do feel that with the right support, everyone has an opportunity to better their lives and circumstances.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect people to the resources they need. We know that searching for help can often feel overwhelming, especially if you're unsure where to start. Through this website, we want to make that process easier by providing a well-organized, easy-to-navigate platform full of helpful information on local services in Las Vegas. Whether it's food assistance, job training, or mental health support, we make it easier for you to find what you need when you need it.

What We Offer

We offer various resources like Health & Wellness resources, Employment & Career resources, Housing Assistance, Financial Assistance & Social Services, Youth & Family Programs, Legal Resources, Community Events & Volunteering. Check out our full list for more resources to choose from.

Why Choose Us?

We know everyone's needs are different, and that is why we work to serve as your one-stop destination for all your questions about the community. The Community Resource Center of Las Vegas has designed this website with the user in mind: easy navigation, easy-to-follow categories, and timely updates on a host of local resources. Whether you are new or have lived in Las Vegas for many years, the Community Resource Center of Las Vegas helps you connect with the right services quickly and efficiently.

Our Pledge

Our commitment is to provide reliable, accurate, and timely information for every person or family to make an informed choice. We team up with local organizations and government agencies, along with community groups, who can ensure that the data we provide is correct and relevant. We regularly update our resources so that any changes to local services will always be current.

Get Involved

We believe information is power, and sharing those resources with others makes for a better community. If you have information about other services or events that would benefit Las Vegas residents, please contact us. We are always looking to add new, helpful resources to our website to better serve the community.
